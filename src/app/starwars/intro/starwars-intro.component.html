The purpose of this order is to list all canon related materials of the Star Wars universe in a way that serves the narrative above a purely chronological approach. It is a good way to discover the Star Wars mythology for all newcomers or to dive into it again for old fans.
This list focus on the rebooted Disney 2015 canon universe but does not include the following materials (either because it does not fit well with the rest of the canon or because it does not add much to it):
  • The legends materials pre 2015 (obviously)
  • The many short stories published in different books and Star Wars Insider magazine
  • The "Life Day" comic oneshot
  • The "Return of the Jedi - Ewoks" comic oneshot
  • The many young reader books published by Disney
  • Most of the Star Wars Adventures comics published by IDW
  • The Forces of Destiny mini episodes
  • The Galaxy of Adventures promotional videos
  • Most video games that do not focus on storytelling
  • The Clone Wars season 4 episodes 5 (Mercy Mission) and 6 (Nomad Droids)
  • The Clone Wars season 5 episodes 10, 11, 12, 13 (D-Squad)
