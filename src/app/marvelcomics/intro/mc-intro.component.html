This is the complete reading order for the Marvel Cosmic Universe.

This reading order contains all of the Marvel comics issues related to the cosmic side of the Marvel universe.
It follows the chronological order of events as much as possible but the main objective is centered on the cohesiveness of the story.
Where to start ?
- Infinity Saga (Thanos Wars)
- Annihilation
- Secret Wars (Hickman's Fantastic Four)
What to read ?
Read everything from top to bottom starting from your chosen starting point.
Pay close attention to the required pre-readings for each story arc to be sure to fully understand all aspect of the story.
Use the issues filter to focus on the issues related to the main storyline of the Marvel cosmic universe. This way you will have a much more coherent story.
Use the collection filter to focus on a character or a team of characters. Not all appearances of that character will be listed, only what is relevant to its own story.
